Wes Chatham is an American actor. He got into acting when he got a role in the 2002 movie Antwone Fisher. After this, he was persuaded to move to Hollywood to further pursue acting. He also played a supporting role in the movie The Help. He went on to portray Castor in The Hunger Games: Mockingjay – Part 1 and Part 2. He’s also known for his role as Amos Burton on the Syfy TV series The Expanse.
