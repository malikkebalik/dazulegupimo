Chrisean Malone

 Posted on July 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  304 words  |  Martina Birk

Chrisean Malone is an American Instagram star, musician and dancer. She's also known as Chrisean Rock. She was born on March 14, 2000, in Baltimore, Maryland.

Family

Chrisean can't boast a happy childhood and family life.

The celebrity has 11 siblings.

Chrisean's dad was arrested at the moment when she was born. Both of her parents were addicted to drugs. There’s not much information about her parents on the web.

Instagram career

She joined Instagram in 2015. She began to post lifestyle content as well as modeling shoots where she was dressed in streetwear and swimwear.

In 2018, she launched a YouTube channel.

In addition to being a model, the star makes rap music. Her boyfriend's label signed her. You might have seen her in music videos.

Personal life

She used to date Johnathan Jamall Porter. His stage name is Blueface, he's a rapper. After they broke up, Chrisean seems to be single and fully concentrated on her career.

They got to know each other thanks to the Blue Girls Clubs reality show on OnlyFans. In this show, Blueface invited women to live in his house for one month. During the show, Chrisean had a fight with Alexis Aiden, Johnathan's ex-girlfriend, and lost her front tooth. Blueface paid for having it replaced.

Facts

  • She won $10,000 in the Ultimate Tag game show.
  • Fashion Nova and Ethika clothing brands have appointed her their ambassadors.
  • While her dad was in prison, her mom couldn't pay the bills. When Chrisean was 9 years old, they became homeless for a few years. Nevertheless, she managed to graduate from a high school in Maryland. In 2020, she got a partner degree in social science and kinesiology.
  • In 2022, she was arrested for driving a stolen car and selling drugs. She was crying on her mugshot. The star was taken into custody.
