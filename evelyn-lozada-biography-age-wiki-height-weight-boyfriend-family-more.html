Moving towards her career, Evelyn took the first step regarding her professional career in the year 2007 by moving to Miami, Florida, where she worked as a secretary by an entertainment attorney. Later, she served as a co-owner of a shoe boutique, Dulce in Coral Gables, Florida.

Furthermore, in the year 2010, Lozada made her television debut by appearing in the reality TV show ‘Basketball Wives’. Apart from that, she made her appearance on the television show Iyanla: Fix My Life in September 2012.

Other than this, Evelyn also starred in Livin’ Lozada with her daughter, Shaniece Hairston in the year 2015. In fact, she has guest appeared in some episodes of different TV series including The Better Show, Hollywood Medium, The Real, Home & Family. Besides this, she also wrote a novel, ‘The Wives Association: Inner Circle’.

Reflecting on her personal life, Evelyn currently seems to be single. However, earlier she was in a romantic relationship with Jamal Hairston. In fact, she also gave birth to his daughter, Shaniece Hairston in 1993. After that, she again entered into a relationship with Antoine Walker in 1998.

However, their relationship came to an end after ten years. Later, in 2010, she started to date footballer Chad ‘Ochocinco’ Johnson. Moreover, the couple got engaged on Nov 16, 2010, and eventually wed in Saint Martin on Jul 4, 2012.

Unfortunately, after a month of marriage, Chad was arrested on charges of domestic violence after head-butting Evelyn during an argument. So, she filed for divorce on Aug 14, 2012, and the divorce was finalized on Sept 19, 2012.

Furthermore, she also got engaged to basketball player Carl Crawford in December 2013. Later, the couple was blessed with a cute son named Carl Leo Crawford on March 22, 2014. But their relation couldn’t last long and ended in August 2017.
