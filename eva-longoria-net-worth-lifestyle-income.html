Eva Longoria | Net Worth & Income

An American TV and film actress Eva Longoria’s net worth is a whopping $80 million as of December 2023.

During the peak of her career, she was the highest-paid TV actress in the world.

In 2011, the actress earned over $13 million within that calendar year, which was enough to make her the highest-paid TV star on the planet.

The actress basically earns the most from her career as an actress, and a few million on her fortune can be attributed to her endorsement deals, investments, and other ventures.

Longoria gradually negotiated a higher salary while appearing in ABC’s comedy-drama series Desperate Housewives.

Reportedly, for the first season of the show, she made a mere $30,000 per episode. Her pay was bumped to $275,000 for the second season, which sounds fair given the popularity of the show.

During this time, Eva renegotiated her pay with the production and sealed a $325,000 deal per episode.

And with backend incentives, the figure rose to an estimated $375,000, which worked out to around $8.625 million per season in base salary.

Even though her salary was increased several times, Eva later noted that the negotiation process wasn’t easy.

She also noted that her pay raise was possible because of the massive success of the show.

Aside from Desperate Housewives, Eva also has a slew of lucrative endorsement deals under her belt, which contributes greatly to her income.

She has endorsed tons of big brands over the course of her career. And let’s not forget her income from movies and other TV shows.

Furthermore, Eva has a superb real estate portfolio. She purchases properties at a lower price and trades them for profit.

Eva Longoria | Houses & Cars

Houses

As mentioned before, Eva has a solid real estate portfolio. In fact, a good chunk of her net worth is in various properties around the nation.

In 2015, Longoria purchased a three-acre compound in Hollywood Hills, formerly owned by Tom Cruise, for a whopping $11.5 million.

She finally offloaded this property in April 2020 for $8.2 million after spending three years trying to sell. She took $3 million in the loss while trading this property.

Similarly, Longoria listed the Mediterranean villa in Hollywood for $1.395 million in 2016. She had purchased the villa back in 2005 for $1.15 million.

She was ultimately able to sell the property for the reduced price of $1.375 million.

Eva also had a beautiful 8,600 square feet mansion in Hollywood Dell, which she purchased back in 2006 for $3.6 million. She sold this one too in 2018 for $3.2 million with a loss of $400,000.

Moreover, Eva purchased a gorgeous 11,000 square feet, 1.42-acre mansion in the hills of Beverly Hills in 2017 for $13.5 million.

Aside from that, she also owns a property in Malibu. She purchased this one in 2008 for $2.5 million.

Furthermore, Longoria has a house in San Antonio which she purchased in 2005.

Cars

Eva Longoria | Charity

Eva Longoria founded her non-profit organization Eva’s Heroes in 2006. The organization helps young adults with developmental disabilities.

It is headquartered in her 3,500 square feet home in San Antonio, Texas.

She was inspired to establish the charity because of her sister, who has intellectual disabilities.

Similarly, Eva serves as a national spokesperson for PADRES Contra EI Cancer.

In 2009, the Hollywood Reported named her Philanthropist of the Year for her commitment to Latino causes.

Moreover, in 2009 Eva appeared on Fort Boyard, helping attract more than €20,000 for the Make-A-Wish Foundation.

Eva was ranked at number 42 in the list of 200 Most Influential Philanthropist and Social Entrepreneurs Worldwide in 2017 by UK-based Company Richtopia.

Eva Longoria | Career

Longoria was born in Corpus Christi, Texas, to Mexican-American parents alongside three sisters.

She was raised in a Roman Catholic household.

During her time in high school, she worked at Wendy’s.

After graduating high school, she obtained her Bachelor of Science in kinesiology from Texas A&M University-Kingsville.

She also won Miss Corpus Christi USA in 1998.

Eva eventually went to Los Angeles and began auditioning for roles.
