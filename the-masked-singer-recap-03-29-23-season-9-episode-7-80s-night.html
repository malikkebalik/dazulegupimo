Tonight on FOX The Masked Singer airs with an all-new Wednesday, March 29, 2023, Season 9 episode 7 and we have your The Masked Singer recap below. On tonight’s The Masked Singer season, 9 episode 7 called “80s Night,” as per the FOX synopsis, “The Masked Singer goes retro with a trip back to the 80s! A brand-new group of singers belt out hit favorites from the neon decade, but only one will move forward in the competition as the other two unmask!”

In tonight’s The Masked Singer episode, its 80s night on The Masked Night! Young MC opens the show with a performance. He introduces the panel who are wearing their best 80s attire. They get right into performances with The Doll kicking off the performances.

Nick introduces the Doll with some clues – a surreal life growing up who met other misfits. The doll sings with the panel surprised to hear a male voice. He sings a well known 80s song, Don’t You Forget About Me.

The panel and the audience is impressed. They get a clue from Erik Estrada of Chips fame. He shares one word “Ghostwriter.” The panel is thinking Sebastan Bach, maybe Gene Simmons. Keen thinks its Donnie Wahlburg. Jenny breaks that theory when she says hello to him in the crowd.

The Scorpion is introduced next. She shares some clues about living in the sticks and working in the food, drink industry to get ahead. She sings a Cyndi Lauper song. The panel thinks she is a dancer and an athletic. After her song, the panel commends her for her performance.

Jenny thinks this is a housewife. It could Be Lisa Rinna or Denise Richards. Ken thinks it might be Heidi Klum. They all laugh. Next up is Moose.

Moose sings with a presence. He doesn’t move much but They love his gritty voice. Dallas actress Charlene Tilton brings a clue to the stage. The clue is “acceptance speech.” The panel is thinking Ed O’Neil, maybe Norm from Cheers.

Its time to decide between the Moose, the Scorpio and the Doll. All three come to the stage. Nick reveals the Moose didn’t make it. He is headed home. The panel is sad to see him go but share their guesses. Kn thinks John Goodman, Nicole thinks Ed O’Neil while Jenny thinks Norm from Cheers, George Wendt. The audience shouts “take it off.”

The Moose is………George Wendt. The audience is thrilled to see him as well as the panel. They move on to the battle royal between the Scorpio and the Doll. They both wow the crowd. The panel really loved their battle.

Its time to reveal the champion. Nick shares that the winner this week is the Doll. They are sad to see the Scorpio go. The panel’s best guesses are Lisa Rinna, Denise Richards, and Heidi Klum. The Scorpio reveals herself to be…..Christina Quinn from Selling Sunset.

THE END!
