She gave eight exceptional full-length studio albums to music enthusiasts. Most of the albums went into top charts in all major music markets. One of his albums ‘Good Girl Gone Bad’ was re-launched in 2008 due to its boundless cosmopolitan success. She also released a remixed version of her music albums at different times. The girl went on many positive music tours to promote her albums. Fans from across the United States keep waiting to see Rihanna performing in a concert.

Who are Rihanna’s Father and Mother?

Rihanna opened her eyes on Feb 20, 1988, in Saint Michael, Barbados. The name of her mother is Monica while the father of the stunning lady is known as Ronald Fenty. Her mother comes from Guyanese descent and father has West Indian and Irish ethnic roots. That’s why the glamorous American singer comes from Afro-Guyanese and Afro-Barbadian ancestries. Rihanna is the elder sister of two handsome brothers named Rajad and Rorrey Fenty. Furthermore, she has a half-brother and two half-sisters from her father’s side. They were born from her father’s former romantic relationships with different women.

She spent most of her childhood in Bridgetown, which is the capital and the largest city of Barbados. Her mother was an accountant by profession. The father of the amazing girl served as a warehouse supervisor. Rihanna lived with her family in a three-bedroom house located in Bridgetown. She used to sell clothes with her father in a street-stall. She faced a dilemma at an early age due to her father who was excessively involved in alcoholism and cocaine addiction. Rihanna saw the father beating her mother day and night. In short, her childhood was affected by family violence.

Music Career

Rihanna proved herself as an amazing singer in a very short time. She began her endeavor in the music industry and soon gained the attention of music labels, producers, and record houses. The debut studio album of the stunner officially launched on Aug 30, 2005. It was released with title ‘Music of the Sun’ through Def Jam Recordings. She was first discovered by the famous record producer known as Evan Rogers. He helped the struggling girl in achieving big milestones in her life.

The second studio album of Rihanna came out with caption ‘A Girl Like Me’ and made every music enthusiast stunned with amazing lyrics. She released her all-time favorite studio album ‘Good Girl Gone Bad’ on May 31, 2007, through SRP Records and Def Jam Recordings. She worked with different producers to make this album a great commercial success. The female singer re-launched an updated version of the album again in 2008. The Fourth studio album of Rihanna came out as ‘Rated R’ on Nov 20, 2009. Music labels that took part in the launch were SRP Records and Def Jam Recordings.

Rihanna didn’t want her fans to wait longer, so she issued her fifth musical collection in form of a full-length studio album ‘Loud’. It was released on Nov 12, 2010, by SRP Records and Def Jam Recordings. Songs of this particular album were recorded from Feb to Aug 2010. She brought her sixth studio album ‘Talk That Talk’ on Nov 18, 2011, through SRP Records and Def Jam Recordings. Rihanna’s seventh studio album was titled ‘Unapologetic’. It went out for public on Nov 19, 2012, by the same record houses. The recording sessions were completed from June to Nov 2012. The eighth studio album of the American celeb was launched under the heading ‘Anti’ on Jan 28, 2016, via Roc Nation and Westbury Road Records.
